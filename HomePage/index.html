About DSC

Developer Student Club at Sanjivani K.B.P. Polytechnic is a community that helps students grow as developers through workshops, hackathons, and real-world projects.

Vision

Our focus is on imparting the knowledge and traning which should enable students to harmonize theory with pratice, concept with application, and problems with solutions. It will prepare them to apply engineering principles, practices and processes to design, develop, deploy and maintain software systems, and growth in development and technical skills.

Mission

Our mission is to develop technology based solutions, products that provide both the means and methods for faster working, and better performance and to achieve more. The technologies we develop provide users the control and functionality needed to be more efficient and we develop the project for real-world use.
